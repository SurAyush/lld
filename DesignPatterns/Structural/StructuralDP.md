## 🧱 What are Structural Design Patterns?

Structural patterns deal with **how classes and objects are put together** to form larger structures.

They help when:

* You want to **reuse existing classes**
* You want to **add new behavior without changing old code**
* You want to **wrap, adapt, or organize objects**

### In simple terms:

> Structural patterns are about **building blocks and how you assemble them**.
